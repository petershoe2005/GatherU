Go-to-Market

The campus domination playbook

1

Campus Ambassador Program

Recruit 5 student ambassadors per campus. Incentivize with commission on every referral's first sale.

2

Move-In / Move-Out Blitz

Launch campaigns at the start and end of each semester when trading volume peaks (Aug, Jan, May).

3

Student Org Partnerships

Partner with Greek life, clubs, and student government for co-branded promotions and fundraiser auctions.

4

Viral Campus → Campus

Once one school hits critical mass, students refer friends at other schools. Expand to 10 campuses in Year 1.
